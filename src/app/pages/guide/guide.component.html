Website Overview

The navbar provides quick access to essential sections:

  • Account: Manage your user information and settings.
  • Vehicles: Add and manage company vehicles, view owners, and vehicle details.

How to Use

  1. Login: After logging in, you'll see a world map. The blue dot shows your current location.
  2. Add Vehicles: Go to the "Vehicles" section to add and manage your fleet.
  3. Plan a Route: Use the search bar to select start, middle and end points, then click "Start".
The tool calculates the optimal route based on:
  • Fuel consumption and cost
  • Travel time
  • Number of stops

This tool helps reduce costs, improve efficiency, and minimize environmental impact.
